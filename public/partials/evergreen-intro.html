Evergreen

..when I touched soil and mud, stone
and the sea-foam of my soul,
beings that recognize my footsteps, my words,
the tendrils of green life kissing my mouth,
I said, "Here I am," and I undressed in the brilliance.
I let my hands sink into the sea, and when everything reached transparency,
beneath the earth, I found peace.
Neruda, Ceremonial Songs, "Fiesta's End," XIII.
